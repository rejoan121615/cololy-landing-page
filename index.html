Colonist NFTs

Colonists are an NFT collection of 25,000 unique, programmatically generated space animals that are redeemable for their in-game playable character and cosmetic items in our upcoming MMO on the blockchain.

They come in 5 different species, with a hard-capped supply of 5,000 per species. Each species is dropped one by one, and is only available to mint once the previously dropped species has sold out.

(The whole collection is dropping early September 2021)

Colony Online

Colony Online is a social MMO (Metaverse) on the blockchain where all playable characters and in-game cosmetic items are NFTs.

The game takes place on an alternate-reality Moon Colony, where a society of randomly generated space animals (called Colonists) have colonized the moon and turned it into their very own social playground for chic materialism, party lifestyle, and moon mining.

This world will have its very own cryptocurrency to fuel an economy of in-game item ownership on the blockchain.

2021 roadmap

Here is what to expect after all 25,000 Colonists are sold through. The % on the roadmap does not mean the % of Colonists sold, but rather the % of our roadmap being complete.

  • 20%

    We will airdrop Colonist holders species specific concept art NFTs for different regions of the game map (one per wallet)

  • 40%

    We introduce trait re-rolling, where you will be able to re-roll a singular trait on your Colonist for .0125 ETH with a maximum of 3 re-rolls per Colonist.

  • 60%

    We open our limited quantity merch store on our website which is only accessible for verified owners of Colonists (Metamask sign in)

  • 80%

    For 72 hours, holders of Colonists will be able to mint a “Companion” which is essentially a pet with future utility in Colony Online. (one per Colonist)

  • 100%

    Those who hold at least one Colonist per species (all 5) will receive a powerup boost for one super-charged re-roll that increases their chances of rolling a legendary item.

After we successfully deliver on every roadmap item; We continue full-swing game development of Colony Online (our MMO on the blockchain) and releasing early access to Colonist holders in 2022.
